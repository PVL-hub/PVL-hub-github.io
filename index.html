DotsSlider

Description

DotsSlider is a puzzle game developed on Python Kivy for Android.

You can move the dots up, down, left or right.

If the dot is on the edge of the playing field and you move it in the direction of that boundary, the dot will appear on the opposite side of the playing field.

If there are other dots on the horizontal or vertical lines along with the dot you selected, they will move in the same direction.

The stage will be completed when all the dots are in the pockets.

Game field five sizes: 12, 24, 36, 48 and 60.

Interesting details!

  • If you pass the levels perfectly, then advertising will be less)
  • If you reset the progress, you get a whole new set of stages
